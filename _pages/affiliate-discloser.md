## Our Commitment to Transparency

At LangChain Tutorials, we believe in complete transparency with our readers. This page explains our affiliate relationships and how we may earn commissions from products and services we recommend.

---

## What Are Affiliate Links?

Affiliate links are special tracking URLs that allow us to earn a commission when you purchase a product or service through our recommendation. This is at **no additional cost to you** - you pay the same price whether you use our affiliate link or go directly to the vendor.

### We May Include Affiliate Links For:

- **AI/ML Tools and Services** - LangChain, OpenAI, Anthropic, and other AI platforms
- **Cloud Hosting Providers** - AWS, Google Cloud, Azure, and similar services
- **Development Tools** - IDEs, code editors, and developer utilities
- **Learning Resources** - Online courses, books, and educational platforms
- **Software Services** - APIs, databases, and SaaS products we recommend

### Where You'll Find Them:

- Tutorial content and guides
- Product reviews and comparisons
- Resource recommendations
- "Tools We Use" sections
- Email newsletters (if applicable)

## Our Editorial Standards

### 1. Honest Recommendations Only

We **only** recommend products and services that we:
- Have personally used and tested
- Genuinely believe will benefit our readers
- Would recommend even without affiliate compensation

### 2. No Influence on Content

Affiliate relationships **do not** influence:
- Tutorial quality or accuracy
- Technical recommendations
- Code examples or best practices
- Our honest opinions and reviews

### 3. Balanced Reviews

When reviewing products:
- We highlight both pros and cons
- We compare alternatives fairly
- We disclose if we haven't personally used a product
- We never misrepresent features or capabilities

## Sponsored Content

### Different from Affiliate Links

Sponsored content is when we're paid directly to create content about a product or service. This is **different** from affiliate marketing.

### Our Policy on Sponsored Content

If we publish sponsored content, we will:
- Clearly label it as "Sponsored," "Paid Partnership," or "Advertisement"
